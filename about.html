About Peerless Stores

At Peerless Stores, we are committed to reconnecting people with the tastes of home by providing access to native foods from around the world. From the shores of Africa to the Caribbean and Asia, our mission is to serve communities in the United Kingdom with authentic ingredients that bring families and friends together, bridging cultures and traditions.

Our Vision

To connect people and their native foods.

Our Mission

Connect people by offering their native foods in the most reliable, responsible, and sustainable manner, anywhere, anytime.

Our Motto

For the love of native food.

Who We Are

Peerless Stores is the foremost retailer of native food and drinks in the United Kingdom, serving foods to people from Africa, the Caribbean, Asian communities, and beyond. We are committed to sharing and enriching long-held culinary traditions and maintaining the bond of families and communities far from home.

Our Background

Registered as Peerless Stores Limited, trading as Peerless Stores since 2024, we are based in Wigan, Manchester. Our mission is to connect people by offering their native foods in a reliable, responsible, and sustainable manner, anywhere, anytime in the UK. We bring diverse ethnic communities the highest quality products at the best possible prices.

Our Products and Services

We specialize in selling and distributing ethnic foods and products. Our offerings include both online and in-store purchases. Online orders are available with next-day UK delivery, following standard terms and conditions. We continuously expand our range of quality foods and drinks, sold at very competitive prices to reach a broader market.

Our specialties include fresh exotic native foods and drinks, such as groceries, grains, flours, fruits and vegetables, exotic frozen seafood, poultry, and meat. Please visit our Products Page for more details.
